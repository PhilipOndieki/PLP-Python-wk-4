Power seeks not justification but expression, like a flame that does not ask why it burns.  
He who gazes too long into the abyss discovers that the abyss has already written his reflection.  
The spirit must shed its comfortable illusions before it can dance freely upon the mountains.  
Morality is often a mask worn by weakness, pretending to be strength.  
Only those who dare to create new values escape the prison built by old truths.
